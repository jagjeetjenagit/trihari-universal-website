Step 5: Troubleshooting Checklist

If submissions are still not working, check these:

  1. Google Form Settings:
    • Is "Accepting responses" turned ON?
    • Is "Collect email addresses" turned ON or OFF?
    • Are there any restrictions on who can respond?
  2. Form URL: Make sure the form ID hasn't changed
  3. Entry IDs: Verify by inspecting the actual Google Form source
  4. Required Fields: Make sure all required fields in Google Form have corresponding entries

Step 6: Check Google Form Responses

After testing, check your Google Form responses:

  1. Go to your Google Form
  2. Click on the "Responses" tab
  3. See if any test submissions appear
  4. Check the timestamp to match your test submissions

Expected Results: You should see entries like "Basic Test User" and "Full Test User" if the tests worked.
