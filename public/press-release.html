Trihari Universal Launches Revolutionary Digital Audition Platform

New platform streamlines casting process, making auditions more accessible for talent across India

Mumbai, India - September 29, 2025 - Trihari Universal, a premier film production house known for creating cinematic stories with bold visuals, today announced the launch of its revolutionary digital audition platform at trihariuniversal.com.

Transforming the Audition Experience

The new platform represents a significant advancement in how casting directors and talent connect, featuring:

  • Streamlined Applications: Comprehensive digital forms capturing all essential talent information
  • Professional Photo Uploads: High-quality image submission system with cloud storage
  • Instant Notifications: Real-time email updates for both applicants and casting directors
  • Mobile-First Design: Optimized experience across all devices

Industry Impact

"This platform addresses the evolving needs of the entertainment industry," said a spokesperson for Trihari Universal. "We're eliminating traditional barriers and creating opportunities for talent across India to showcase their abilities professionally."

Technical Excellence

Built with cutting-edge technology, the platform features:

  • Advanced form validation and security
  • Professional cloud-based photo storage
  • Automated data management systems
  • Mobile-responsive design for accessibility

About Trihari Universal

Trihari Universal is a full-service film production house specializing in cinematic storytelling with bold visual narratives. The company creates films, commercials, and branded content that resonates with diverse audiences. Founded with a vision to push creative boundaries, Trihari Universal combines traditional storytelling with modern production techniques.

Availability

The digital audition platform is now live and accepting applications at https://trihariuniversal.com. Aspiring actors and talent can submit their applications immediately.
